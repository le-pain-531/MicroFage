Who We Are

About MicroFage

A collective of driven students building real-world web solutions.

Students from Singapore's Finest Institutions

We are a team of motivated students from Raffles Institution, Hwa Chong Institution, and NUS High School of Mathematics and Science who share a common passion for web development and digital design.

Driven by curiosity and the desire to gain real-world experience, we create professional, modern websites for businesses and individuals. Every project we take on is an opportunity to push our skills further and deliver exceptional results.

Our mission is simple: deliver high-quality, affordable websites that help businesses establish a strong online presence — all while we learn, grow, and innovate together.
